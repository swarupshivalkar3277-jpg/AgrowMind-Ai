# Summer Season Advisory

# Overview

The summer season is characterized by high temperatures, intense solar radiation, increased evaporation, and frequent moisture stress. During this period, crops, livestock, soil, and water resources face significant stress.

Effective summer season management helps farmers:

* Protect crops from heat stress
* Conserve water
* Maintain soil moisture
* Reduce yield losses
* Improve livestock welfare
* Enhance farm resilience

Summer advisories focus on efficient water use, heat management, and crop protection.

---

# Characteristics of the Summer Season

## Weather Conditions

Summer conditions typically include:

* High temperatures
* Low humidity in some regions
* High evaporation rates
* Hot winds
* Heat waves
* Water scarcity

---

## Major Challenges

Farmers may face:

* Drought
* Heat stress
* Water shortages
* Pest outbreaks
* Livestock stress
* Soil moisture depletion

---

# Impact of Summer on Agriculture

## Crop Effects

High temperatures may cause:

* Wilting
* Flower drop
* Fruit drop
* Reduced photosynthesis
* Poor pollination

---

## Soil Effects

Summer heat can lead to:

* Moisture loss
* Reduced microbial activity
* Soil cracking

---

## Water Resource Effects

Increased evaporation may reduce:

* Pond water levels
* Reservoir storage
* Groundwater availability

---

# Heat Stress in Crops

## What is Heat Stress?

Heat stress occurs when temperatures exceed the crop's optimum growth range.

---

## Common Symptoms

* Leaf rolling
* Leaf scorching
* Wilting
* Reduced flowering
* Poor fruit set
* Stunted growth

---

## Severe Symptoms

* Flower abortion
* Fruit cracking
* Premature maturity
* Plant death

Water should be used carefully.

Recommended methods:

* Drip irrigation

# Summer Emergency Advisory

### During Heatwaves

* Increase irrigation frequency if water is available.
* Use mulching extensively.
* Protect livestock from direct sunlight.

### During Severe Water Shortage

* Prioritize high-value crops.
* Use life-saving irrigation.
* Reduce water wastage.

### During Crop Stress

* Apply foliar nutrition.
* Monitor pest outbreaks.
* Avoid unnecessary field operations.
