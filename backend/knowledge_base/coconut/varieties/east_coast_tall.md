# East Coast Tall Coconut Variety

## Overview

East Coast Tall (ECT) is one of the most widely cultivated tall coconut varieties in India. It is known for its hardiness, adaptability, long lifespan, and suitability for copra and oil production.

This variety is extensively grown in coastal and inland coconut-growing regions because of its:

* High yield potential
* Strong growth habit
* Tolerance to varying environmental conditions
* Long productive life

East Coast Tall is commonly used in commercial coconut farming and hybrid breeding programs.

---

# Origin and Distribution

## Origin

East Coast Tall originated along the eastern coastal regions of India.

---

## Major Growing Areas

The variety is widely cultivated in:

* Tamil Nadu
* Andhra Pradesh
* Odisha
* Karnataka
* Kerala
* Maharashtra coastal regions

It adapts well to both coastal and inland environments.

* Good annual nut yield

# Climatic Requirements

East Coast Tall grows best under:

* Warm humid tropical climate
* Adequate sunlight
* Moderate to high rainfall

The variety can tolerate varying climatic conditions better than many dwarf varieties.

---

# Soil Requirements

Suitable soils include:

* Sandy loam
* Coastal alluvial soil
* Red loam
* Well-drained lateritic soil

Proper drainage is important.

---

# Irrigation Requirements

Regular irrigation improves:

* Flowering
* Nut setting
* Yield
* Palm health

Irrigation is especially important during:

* Summer months
* Drought periods

Drip irrigation is highly beneficial.

---

# Nutrient Management

Balanced nutrition is essential for high productivity.

## Recommended Practices

* Apply organic manure regularly
* Use balanced fertilizers
* Provide micronutrients when required
* Conduct periodic soil testing

# Pest and Disease Susceptibility

East Coast Tall may be affected by:

## Common Pests

* Rhinoceros beetle
* Red palm weevil
* Eriophyid mite

## Common Diseases

* Bud rot
* Stem bleeding
* Root wilt disease

Integrated pest and disease management is recommended.
