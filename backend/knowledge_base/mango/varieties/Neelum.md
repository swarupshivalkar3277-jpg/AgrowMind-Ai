# Neelum Mango

## Overview

Neelum is a popular late-season mango variety grown mainly in South India. It is valued for its good shelf life, consistent yield, and sweet-tangy flavor. The variety is widely cultivated in Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, and Telangana.

Neelum is also known for its ability to extend the mango season, making it commercially important.

---

# Scientific Classification

Common Name: Neelum Mango

Botanical Name: Mangifera indica L.

Family: Anacardiaceae

---

# Origin

Neelum is believed to have originated in:

- South India (widely cultivated in Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh)

It is now grown in:

- Tamil Nadu
- Andhra Pradesh
- Karnataka
- Telangana
- Some parts of Maharashtra

# Tree Characteristics

## Growth Habit

Characteristics:

- Medium-sized tree
- Dense canopy
- Strong branching structure
- Regular bearing variety

---

## Tree Height

Mature trees may reach:

- 8–12 meters

Can be managed with pruning for commercial orchards.

# Fruit Characteristics

## Fruit Shape

Characteristics:

- Oval to slightly oblong
- Medium-sized fruits
- Smooth skin

---

## Fruit Weight

Average fruit weight:

- 150–250 grams

---

## Skin Color

At maturity:

- Golden yellow
- Sometimes slight greenish tint remains

---

## Pulp Characteristics

Features:

- Deep yellow pulp
- Medium fiber content
- Sweet with mild tanginess
- Firm texture

---

## Taste and Aroma

Neelum is known for:

- Balanced sweet and tangy taste
- Mild but pleasant aroma
- Refreshing flavor

---

# Flowering and Fruiting

## Flowering Season

Occurs during:

- January to March

---

## Harvest Season

Harvesting period:

- June to August

Neelum is a late-season mango variety.

---

# Climatic Requirements

Ideal conditions:

- Tropical and subtropical climate
- Temperature: 24°C to 32°C
- Performs well in warm humid regions

---

# Soil Requirements

Suitable soils:

- Well-drained loamy soils
- Red soils
- Lateritic soils

Preferred pH:

- 5.5 to 7.5

Avoid waterlogging conditions.

---

# Yield Potential

Average yield:

- 100–200 kg per mature tree annually

Yield depends on:

- Orchard management
- Nutrient application
- Irrigation
- Pest control

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Why is Neelum mango important?

Because it extends the mango season and provides late harvest supply.

## Is Neelum a sweet mango?

Yes, it has a sweet-tangy balanced flavor.

## When is Neelum mango harvested?

Generally from June to August.

## Is Neelum good for commercial farming?

Yes, due to its regular yield and good shelf life.
