# Alphonso Mango

## Overview

Alphonso mango is one of the most famous and commercially valuable mango varieties in India. It is renowned worldwide for its rich flavor, attractive color, smooth texture, and exceptional aroma.

Often referred to as the "King of Mangoes," Alphonso is highly preferred in domestic and export markets due to its premium fruit quality.

---

# Scientific Classification

Common Name: Alphonso Mango

Botanical Name: Mangifera indica L.

Family: Anacardiaceae

---

# Origin

Alphonso mango is believed to have originated in western India and is extensively cultivated in:

- Maharashtra
- Goa
- Karnataka
- Gujarat

Major production areas include:

- Ratnagiri
- Devgad
- Sindhudurg
- Raigad

---

# Tree Characteristics

## Growth Habit

Characteristics:

- Medium to large-sized tree.
- Dense canopy.
- Vigorous growth.

---

## Tree Height

Mature trees may reach:

- 8–15 meters under normal conditions.

Canopy management is recommended for commercial orchards.

---

# Fruit Characteristics

## Fruit Shape

Characteristics:

- Oval to oblong.
- Symmetrical appearance.
- Attractive shape.

---

## Fruit Weight

Average fruit weight:

- 150–300 grams

Depends on orchard management and growing conditions.

---

## Skin Color

At maturity:

- Golden yellow.
- May develop a reddish blush on the shoulder.

---

## Pulp

Characteristics:

- Deep saffron-yellow color.
- Smooth texture.
- Nearly fiberless.

---

## Flavor

Known for:

- Rich sweetness.
- Pleasant aroma.
- Excellent taste.

---

# Maturity and Harvesting

## Harvest Season

Typical harvesting period:

- March to June

Peak season varies by region.

---

## Maturity Indicators

Signs of maturity:

- Shoulder development.
- Color change.
- Full fruit size.
- Characteristic aroma.

---

# Climatic Requirements

Ideal conditions:

### Temperature

- 24°C to 30°C

### Rainfall

- 750–2500 mm annually

### Humidity

Moderate humidity is preferred.

Excessive rainfall during flowering may affect fruit set.

---

# Soil Requirements

Suitable soils:

- Well-drained loamy soils.
- Lateritic soils.
- Sandy loam soils.

Preferred soil pH:

- 5.5 to 7.5

Avoid:

- Waterlogged conditions.

---

# Flowering Characteristics

Flowering period:

- December to February

Flowering may vary depending on climate and location.

---

# Yield Potential

Average yield:

- 100–300 fruits per mature tree annually.

Yield depends on:

- Tree age.
- Nutrition management.
- Irrigation.
- Pest and disease control.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Why is Alphonso called the King of Mangoes?

Because of its exceptional flavor, aroma, smooth texture, and high market value.

## Which state is famous for Alphonso mango?

Maharashtra, especially Ratnagiri and Devgad regions.

## Is Alphonso suitable for export?

Yes. Alphonso is one of India's most important export mango varieties.

## What is the average fruit weight of Alphonso?

Generally between 150 and 300 grams depending on growing conditions.
