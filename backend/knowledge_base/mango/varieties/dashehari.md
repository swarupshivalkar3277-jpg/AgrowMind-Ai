# Dashehari Mango

## Overview

Dashehari is one of the most popular and widely cultivated mango varieties in North India. It is known for its excellent aroma, sweet taste, fiberless pulp, and early-mid season maturity.

It is highly valued in domestic markets and is considered one of the best eating-quality mango varieties.

---

# Scientific Classification

Common Name: Dashehari Mango

Botanical Name: Mangifera indica L.

Family: Anacardiaceae

---

# Origin

Dashehari mango is believed to have originated in:

- Malihabad region, near Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India

Today it is widely grown in:

- Uttar Pradesh
- Bihar
- Haryana
- Punjab
- Madhya Pradesh

Malihabad remains one of the most famous production belts.

---

# Tree Characteristics

## Growth Habit

Characteristics:

- Medium-sized tree
- Dense canopy
- Upright growth pattern

---

## Tree Height

Mature trees may reach:

- 8–12 meters

Can be managed easily with pruning and canopy management.

---

# Fruit Characteristics

## Fruit Shape

Characteristics:

- Long and oblong shape
- Slight curve in some fruits
- Smooth skin

---

## Fruit Weight

Average fruit weight:

- 150–250 grams

Smaller than premium export varieties but high in quality.

---

## Skin Color

At maturity:

- Greenish-yellow
- Light yellow when fully ripe

---

## Pulp Characteristics

Features:

- Yellow pulp
- Very sweet taste
- Fiberless texture
- Highly aromatic

---

## Taste and Aroma

Dashehari is famous for:

- Strong, pleasant aroma
- Very sweet flavor
- Juicy pulp

---

# Flowering and Fruiting

## Flowering Season

Occurs during:

- February to March

---

## Harvest Season

Harvesting period:

- June to July

One of the mid-season mango varieties.

---

# Climatic Requirements

Ideal conditions:

- Warm tropical to subtropical climate
- Temperature: 24°C to 32°C
- Moderate humidity

Excess rainfall during flowering may reduce yield.

---

# Soil Requirements

Suitable soils:

- Well-drained sandy loam soils
- Alluvial soils of North India

Preferred pH:

- 5.5 to 7.5

Avoid waterlogging.

---

# Yield Potential

Average yield:

- 80–150 kg per tree (mature trees)

Yield depends on:

- Orchard management
- Tree age
- Nutrient availability
- Pest control

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Why is Dashehari mango famous?

Because of its excellent aroma, sweet taste, and fiberless pulp.

## Where is Dashehari mango grown?

Mainly in Uttar Pradesh, especially Malihabad region.

## Is Dashehari a good mango variety?

Yes, it is considered one of the best tasting mango varieties in India.

## What is the fruit size of Dashehari mango?

Generally 150–250 grams per fruit.
